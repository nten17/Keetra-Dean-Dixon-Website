    About

    Keetra is an experiemntal graphic designer who makes thoughtful and inspriing work. It aims to have a social commentary while also being visually appealing and holding this aestethic of multiples and layering that is found in a lot of her work.

    The function of the layering points to the multiple, deep layers of society and the understanding of language as we use it. I appreciate this and think that it enhaces her work. The experience is made better by the layered meaning (literally) that Keetra uses in her pieces. Her unique blend of meaning and visuals make for compelling works.

    She also keeps a studio space on the side JK Keller.
